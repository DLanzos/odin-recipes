Rice with Milk

Description

Here you're going to learn how to make this traditional dish from Argentina.

Get what is needed, follow the instructions and wnjoy it.

Ingredients:

  • White rice
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Directions:

  1. Put the milk into a cooking pot, add the sugar, stir, heat the milk until it starts to boil.
  2. Add the rice, keep stiring until the rice turns soft.
  3. Wait for it to cool down.
  4. Serve it puting some cinnamon on top of it.
